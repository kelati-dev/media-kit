Who I Am

KELATI

I'm Kelati — a Seattle-based content creator covering the music, food, events, and culture that the Pacific Northwest actually lives in. I run @truecounterfeit across TikTok, YouTube, and Instagram.

My content is built on real experience: I'm at the art walks, in the venues, at the restaurant on day one. I don't review what I'm told to — I cover what my city is actually talking about.

From Seahawks championship nights at Lumen Field to Smash burgers in the Fremont, to local music shows and streetwear drops — this is the Seattle content gap I'm filling.

I'm growing fast and actively partnering with brands that genuinely fit my audience. If you're in events, food, music, sports, or lifestyle in the PNW — let's talk.
